This Website!

My love for programming first ignited at the beginning of my A Level Curriculum. I began brainstorming ideas for my Computer Science Project. In fact, this very website is a module of my project, functioning as a portfolio and gateway to my work.

Down Bad

Spelunky is a 2D platformer game created by Derek Yu. It procedurally generates its levels, meaning that the experience is dictated by AI. Taking inspiration from this eye-opening concept, I am currently working on develop a 2D platformer named "Down Bad". It will also focus on procedural generation and mirror Derek Yu's work. The aim of this project is to absorb and retain new knowledge and information regarding software development and how it should be practised. It is also a part of my A Level Project.

THREE JS Car Play Ground

Whilst attending a work experience week at a software engineering company "CoreBlue", I had a conversation with one of the software engineers. He introduced me to THREE JS, which was a relatively new and exciting field at the time. We looked at Bruno Simon's webpage, which fascinated me due to its simplicity and function as a portfolio as well as a demonstration of his programming prowess. Entranced by the idea, I decided to create my own version of his website: a car playground developed using THREE JS.
